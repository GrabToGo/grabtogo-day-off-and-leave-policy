Day Off & Sick Leave Policy

Everything you need to know about working days, weekly offs, planned leave, and sick leave at GrabToGo. Please read carefully and follow the process for requesting time off.

Day Off Policy

  • 🗓️ Total working days: 24 days per month.
  • ☀️ Weekly off: Every Sunday.
  • ➕ Additional leave: Employees may take up to 2 days off per month.
  • 📨 Planned day off must be informed and approved at least 1 week in advance.
  • 📞 Sick leave should be informed to the reporting manager as early as possible before shift start.
  • ⚠️ Repeated last-minute leave requests may affect scheduling and performance review.
⚠
Always inform your manager. Any time off — planned or unplanned — must be communicated to your reporting manager. Do not assume a day off is approved until you hear back.
🤒

Sick Leave Policy

  • 📞 Employees must inform their reporting manager as early as possible if unable to attend work due to illness.
  • ⏰ Sick leave should preferably be informed before shift start time.
  • ✅ Up to 2 sick leave days per month are paid (subject to approval).
  • 💸 Any sick leave exceeding 2 days per month will be considered unpaid leave.
  • 📄 A medical certificate may be requested for extended sick leave.
  • 🚫 Uninformed absence will be treated as unpaid leave.
✕
Uninformed absence is unpaid. If you do not inform your manager and simply don't show up, the day will be marked as unpaid leave regardless of the reason.
🤝

Customer Appointments on Leave Days

🤝 If you have appointments with customers

If you are taking a day off or are unwell and you have scheduled appointments or visits with vendors/customers, you are responsible for making sure those appointments are not missed.

You must either:

• Inform the customer in advance and reschedule the appointment, OR
• Arrange for someone else to attend on your behalf so the vendor is not left waiting.

A missed vendor appointment reflects poorly on GrabToGo and can cost us a vendor. Always close the loop before you take leave.

✓
Best practice: As soon as you know you'll be off, check your schedule for any vendor appointments that day, notify your manager, and either reschedule with the vendor or hand the visit over to a teammate.
📝

How to Apply for Leave

📝 Applying for leave is compulsory

Every leave — planned day off or sick leave — must be applied for through the official GrabToGo Leave Application Form. Filling this form is mandatory for all associates. A leave is not considered approved until you submit the form AND inform your reporting manager.

You must do both of the following:

1. Submit the Leave Application Form (link below)
2. Inform your reporting manager directly (call or message) — planned leave at least 1 week in advance, sick leave as early as possible before your shift
